<p><p>The garnet market is primarily divided into two types: Almandine and Pyrope. Almandine garnet, known for its deep red color, is widely used in abrasives and industrial applications due to its hardness and durability. In contrast, Pyrope garnet exhibits a vibrant red hue, often sought after in the jewelry market for its aesthetic appeal. Both types serve distinct purposes, with Almandine dominating industrial sectors, while Pyrope garnet is preferred for ornamental uses, influencing market dynamics and consumer preferences.</p></p>
