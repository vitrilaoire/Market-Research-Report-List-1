<p><p>Fly ash bricks are eco-friendly construction materials made from fly ash, a byproduct of coal combustion. **Class F Fly Ash Bricks** are made from low-calcium fly ash, offering high strength and durability, ideal for structural applications. **Class C Fly Ash Bricks** use high-calcium fly ash, providing self-cementing properties and resistance to moisture, suitable for harsh environments. Both types reduce waste and carbon emissions, with Class F being more common in regions with low-calcium coal and Class C in areas with high-calcium coal.</p></p>
