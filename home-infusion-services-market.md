<p><p>Home infusion services encompass a range of treatments administered at home, primarily focusing on intravenous therapy and subcutaneous methods. Intravenous therapy involves delivering medications directly into the bloodstream through a vein, ensuring rapid absorption and efficacy for conditions like infections or cancer. Subcutaneous infusion delivers fluids or medications beneath the skin, ideal for patients requiring long-term support, such as those with chronic illnesses. Other methods may include oral or enteral feeds, providing comprehensive care options tailored to individual patient needs.</p></p>
