<p><p>The residential wood flooring market primarily consists of single-layer and multi-layer wood flooring types. Single-layer wood flooring consists of solid, one-piece boards that offer durability and natural aesthetics but can be sensitive to humidity changes. In contrast, multi-layer wood flooring features a core made of multiple layers, often combining wood and other materials for enhanced stability and moisture resistance. This type can better withstand changes in temperature and humidity, making it popular for various residential applications.</p></p>
