<p><p>The nano-mechanical testing market encompasses various techniques to assess material properties at the nanoscale. Nano indentation measures hardness and elastic modulus by applying a precise load using a sharp indenter. Nano scratch testing evaluates scratch resistance and adhesion through controlled abrasive forces. Nano-impact testing assesses material response to impact forces, while nano-tribological testing examines friction and wear at nanoscale contacts. Other methods include fatigue testing and dynamic mechanical analysis, broadening the scope of mechanical characterization for advanced materials.</p></p>
