<p><p>The ventilating storm doors market can be categorized into upper vent storm doors and lower vent storm doors. In contrast, lower vent storm doors have ventilation positioned at the bottom, providing airflow while maintaining privacy and security. Both types enhance energy efficiency and protect against inclement weather, appealing to homeowners seeking improved comfort and aesthetics.</p></p>
