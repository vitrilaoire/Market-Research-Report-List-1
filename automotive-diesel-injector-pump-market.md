<p><p>The automotive diesel injector pump market comprises various types based on design and functionality. The Common Rail Diesel Injection Pump delivers fuel to multiple injectors at high pressure, promoting efficiency and reduced emissions. In-Line Diesel Injection Pumps feature a series of pumps arranged in a line, widely used in heavy-duty applications for their reliability. Distributor (Rotary) Diesel Injection Pumps distribute fuel to multiple cylinders through a rotating mechanism, beneficial for compact engine designs. Additionally, "Others" encompasses innovative or specialized pumps tailored for specific applications.</p></p>
