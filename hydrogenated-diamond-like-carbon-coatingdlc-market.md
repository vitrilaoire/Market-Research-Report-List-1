The market for DLC coatings is primarily divided into three types: Physical Vapor Deposition (PVD), which uses vaporization to create thin films; Plasma-Enhanced Chemical Vapor Deposition (PECVD), where plasma aids in film growth at lower temperatures; and other techniques that include laser deposition and sputtering. Applications span automotive, aerospace, and electronics, driving demand for these durable coatings.</p></p>
