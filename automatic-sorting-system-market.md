<p><p>Automatic sorting systems are essential in logistics and warehousing, facilitating the efficient handling of goods. Linear sortation systems operate in a straight-line configuration, typically using conveyors to direct items to designated locations, ideal for high-speed sorting. In contrast, loop sortation systems employ a circular track, allowing continuous flow and minimizing space requirements. Both market types cater to varying operational needs, enhancing productivity and accuracy in sorting processes across various industries, including e-commerce and retail.</p></p>
