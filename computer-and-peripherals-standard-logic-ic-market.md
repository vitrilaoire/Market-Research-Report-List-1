<p><p>The Computer and Peripherals Standard Logic IC market includes TTL, CMOS, and BiCMOS logic IC types. TTL ICs utilize bipolar transistors, offering fast switching speeds and robustness, making them popular in older applications. CMOS ICs, known for low power consumption and high density, dominate modern electronics. BiCMOS ICs combine the advantages of both technologies, providing high speed with low power usage, suitable for mixed-signal applications. This diversity allows for a wide range of applications in computing and peripheral devices.</p></p>
