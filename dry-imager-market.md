<p><ul><li>Dry Laser Imager</li><li>Dry Thermal Imager</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dry imager market encompasses two main types: dry laser imagers and dry thermal imagers. Dry laser imagers utilize laser technology to produce high-quality images on specialized films without the need for wet processing, offering rapid and efficient imaging solutions, particularly in medical settings. On the other hand, dry thermal imagers employ thermal printing technology to create images, known for their compact design and ease of use, making them ideal for portable applications and environments requiring quick imaging output.</p></p>
