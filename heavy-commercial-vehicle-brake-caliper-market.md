<p><ul><li>Single Piston Caliper</li><li>Multi-Piston Caliper</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Heavy Commercial Vehicle Brake Caliper Market is primarily divided into two types: single piston calipers and multi-piston calipers. Single piston calipers, featuring a single movable piston, are typically lighter and simpler, making them cost-effective for basic braking needs. In contrast, multi-piston calipers are equipped with multiple pistons, providing superior clamping force and improved heat dissipation, resulting in enhanced braking performance and safety. This enables them to cater to heavier loads and more demanding driving conditions.</p></p>
