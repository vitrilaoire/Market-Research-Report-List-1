<p><p>The high-strength medical adhesive market is segmented into soft tissue and hard tissue adhesives. Soft tissue adhesives are designed for applications involving skin, muscles, and organs, providing strong adhesion while allowing flexibility and accommodating movements. Hard tissue adhesives, on the other hand, are formulated for use in stronger bond requirements, such as in bone fixation or dental applications, ensuring durability and stability in load-bearing scenarios. Each type addresses specific medical needs, ensuring optimal healing and patient safety.</p></p>
