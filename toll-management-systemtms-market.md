<p><p>A Toll Management System (TMS) automates toll collection processes, enhancing efficiency and reducing operational costs for toll authorities. The market for TMS can be divided into two main types: on-premise and cloud-based solutions. On-premise systems require local infrastructure and maintenance, offering complete control but higher upfront costs. In contrast, cloud-based TMS solutions provide scalability, lower initial investments, and easier maintenance through remote access, catering to a broader range of users and adapting more readily to operational changes.</p></p>
