<p><p>The Lime Sulphur market includes two main types: Soluble Liquid Concentrate (SL) and Suspension Concentrate (SC). SL is a clear liquid formulation that is easily mixed with water, making it convenient for applications in agriculture as a fungicide and insecticide. In contrast, SC is a stable suspension of particles in liquid, providing prolonged efficacy and better adherence to plant surfaces. Both formulations cater to diverse agricultural needs, enhancing crop protection and overall yield while ensuring ease of use for farmers.</p></p>
