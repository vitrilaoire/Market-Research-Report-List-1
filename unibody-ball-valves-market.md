<p><ul><li>Flange Type</li><li>Thread Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Unibody ball valves are available in two main types: flange type and thread type. Flange type valves feature a flat, round disk with bolted connections, ensuring strong sealing and easy installation in piping systems. They are ideal for larger pipelines requiring robust durability. Thread type valves, on the other hand, utilize threaded ends to connect to pipes, providing a compact and lightweight solution suitable for smaller applications. Both types offer reliable flow regulation and are widely used across various industries.</p></p>
