<p><p>The wearable PDA scanner market includes two primary types: 1D imager barcode scanners and 2D imager barcode scanners. 1D imagers are designed for reading traditional linear barcodes, making them suitable for inventory management and retail applications. In contrast, 2D imagers can decode both linear and matrix (QR) codes, allowing for greater versatility. Both types enhance efficiency in data capture, enabling users to streamline operations in logistics, healthcare, and retail environments by facilitating hands-free scanning and real-time data access.</p></p>
