<p><p>The reflective materials market primarily features two types: micro glass bead and micro prismatic reflective materials. Micro glass bead reflective materials utilize tiny glass beads embedded in a surface to reflect light, enhancing visibility and safety in various applications like signage and road markings. In contrast, micro prismatic reflective materials employ a series of micro prisms to direct light more efficiently, offering superior brightness and durability. Both types are crucial in improving visibility and safety in low-light conditions.</p></p>
