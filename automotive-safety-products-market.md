<p><strong>The Automotive Safety Products Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active</li><li>Passive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive safety products market is divided into two main types: active and passive safety systems. Active safety products are designed to prevent accidents from occurring, incorporating technologies such as anti-lock braking systems, electronic stability control, and adaptive cruise control. On the other hand, passive safety systems focus on minimizing injury during an accident, including airbags, seat belts, and crumple zones. Together, these systems enhance vehicle safety, protecting occupants and reducing the likelihood of serious harm on the road.</p></p>
