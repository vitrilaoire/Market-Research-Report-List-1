<p><p>The Boron Steel market can be categorized into two main types based on boron content. Rigid Boron Steel, containing 0.001%-0.005% boron, is known for its superior strength and durability, making it suitable for applications requiring high structural integrity. In contrast, Brittle Boron Steel, with boron content above 0.007%, tends to exhibit increased brittleness, making it less ideal for structural components but potentially useful in specific high-hardness applications. These categories cater to diverse industrial requirements, emphasizing different performance characteristics.</p></p>
