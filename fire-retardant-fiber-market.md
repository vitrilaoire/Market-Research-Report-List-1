<p><strong>The Fire Retardant Fiber Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Phosphorus-based Fire Retardant Fiber</li><li>Silica-based Fire Retardant Fiber</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fire retardant fiber market is categorized mainly into phosphorus-based and silica-based fibers. Phosphorus-based fire retardant fibers incorporate phosphorus compounds that impede combustion by forming a protective char layer when exposed to heat. These fibers are commonly used in textiles and construction materials. Silica-based fire retardant fibers, on the other hand, leverage the thermal stability of silica to resist flame and heat, making them ideal for use in high-temperature applications like firefighting gear and industrial insulation.</p></p>
