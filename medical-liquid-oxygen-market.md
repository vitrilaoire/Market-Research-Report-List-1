<p><ul><li>On-site Gas</li><li>Bottled Gas</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The medical liquid oxygen market can be divided into two main types: on-site gas and bottled gas. On-site gas systems involve the production of oxygen at healthcare facilities, providing a continuous supply tailored to their needs. In contrast, bottled gas consists of pre-filled cylinders that can be transported and stored for use in various medical applications. Both types ensure the availability of oxygen for patients, but on-site systems offer efficiency and cost-effectiveness, while bottled gas provides flexibility and portability.</p></p>
