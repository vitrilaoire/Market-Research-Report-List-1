<p><p>The Thulium Oxide Sputtering Target market is categorized into two main types: rotatable transformation and non-rotating types. Rotatable targets allow for a continuous and uniform deposition of material, enhancing efficiency and minimizing waste by rotating during the sputtering process. In contrast, non-rotating targets are fixed and rely on a static position for material deposition, often leading to limited uniformity but potentially lower initial costs. Both types are crucial for various applications in electronics, optics, and materials science.</p></p>
