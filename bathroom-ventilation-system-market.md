<p><p>The bathroom ventilation system market is categorized into several types based on fan design. Centrifugal fans are known for high-pressure flow and are effective in longer duct runs. Axial fans offer efficient airflow in compact spaces and are ideal for direct exhaust applications. Mixed flow fans combine the benefits of both, providing a balance of high-pressure and high-volume airflow. Additionally, the "Others" category includes specialized systems like inline fans and heat recovery ventilators, catering to diverse ventilation needs.</p></p>
