<p><p>The ultrasonic thru-beam sensor market consists primarily of two types: emitters and receivers. Emitters generate ultrasonic waves, sending them toward a designated target. When these waves encounter an object, they are reflected back. Receivers capture these reflected waves. Together, they enable object detection, proximity sensing, and safety applications. The market is driven by advancements in automation and safety technology across industries, including manufacturing, logistics, and automotive, where reliable sensing solutions are essential for operational efficiency.</p></p>
