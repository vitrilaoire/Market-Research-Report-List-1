<p><p>The Ortho and Osteobiologics market includes various types of bone graft materials used in orthopedic procedures. Synthetic bone grafts are man-made materials that mimic natural bone properties, offering customization and reduced disease transmission risk. Allografts are bone tissues harvested from human donors, providing a biological scaffold for healing. Blocks and strips refer to specific shaped grafts used for augmenting bone volume and supporting implants. Other categories encompass various innovative products and tissue engineering solutions that enhance bone regeneration and healing.</p></p>
