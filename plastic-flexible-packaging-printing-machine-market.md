<p><p>The Plastic Flexible Packaging Printing Machine market primarily includes two main printing types: Ink Jet and Gravure. Ink Jet printing utilizes droplets of ink to create images and text, allowing for high flexibility and quick changes in designs, making it ideal for short runs and customization. Gravure printing, on the other hand, uses engraved cylinders to transfer ink onto packaging, offering superior image quality and consistency for large volume production. Each method caters to different packaging needs and product types.</p></p>
