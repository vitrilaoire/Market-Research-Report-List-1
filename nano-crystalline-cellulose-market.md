<p><p>The Nano Crystalline Cellulose (NCC) market is primarily categorized by the methods used for its extraction. The Sulfuric Acid Hydrolysis Method involves treating cellulose with concentrated sulfuric acid to break it down into nano-sized crystals, resulting in high purity and yield. In contrast, the Enzymatic Hydrolysis Method employs specific enzymes to selectively degrade cellulose into nanocrystals, offering a more environmentally friendly approach. Both methods contribute to the growing demand for NCC across various industries, including packaging, composites, and pharmaceuticals.</p></p>
