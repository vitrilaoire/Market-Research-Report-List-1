<p><p>Distributed Temperature Sensing (DTS) systems utilize fiber optic cables to monitor temperature across extensive areas. The market is divided into two main types: Single-Mode and Multi-Mode. Single-Mode DTS systems use a narrow fiber, allowing for longer transmission distances and higher sensitivity, ideal for critical applications. In contrast, Multi-Mode DTS employs thicker fibers, offering a broader range but shorter distances, making it suitable for applications where high resolution over shorter lengths is sufficient. Both types cater to diverse industrial needs.</p></p>
