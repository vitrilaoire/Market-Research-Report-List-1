<p><p>Continuous Glucose Monitoring (CGM) systems for diabetics are designed to provide real-time glucose level tracking. The market can be segmented into wearable and implantable types. Wearable CGMs consist of external devices, typically adhered to the skin, featuring sensors that relay glucose data via Bluetooth to smartphones or receivers. Implantable CGMs involve devices inserted under the skin for longer-term monitoring, allowing for less frequent replacements and providing consistent data over extended periods. Both types aim to enhance diabetes management.</p></p>
