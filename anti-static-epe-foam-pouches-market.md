Low Density Polyethylene (LDPE) provides excellent flexibility and cushioning, perfect for lightweight products. Linear Low Density Polyethylene (LLDPE) combines elasticity and strength, making it versatile for both protective and wrapping uses, ensuring enhanced static protection for sensitive electronics.</p></p>
