<p><p>The flat laser cutting machine market consists of two main types: CO2 flat laser cutting machines and fiber flat laser cutting machines. CO2 machines utilize a gas laser to effectively cut non-metal materials and thicker metals, making them suitable for applications like wood and acrylic cutting. Fiber machines, on the other hand, use solid-state lasers that offer higher efficiency, quicker cutting speeds, and precision, making them ideal for metals such as steel and aluminum in industrial settings.</p></p>
