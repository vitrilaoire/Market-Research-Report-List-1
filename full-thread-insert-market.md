<p><p>Full Thread Insert market types refer to specific thread configurations used in various applications. "T-ISO" refers to ISO metric threads, while "T-NPT" denotes National Pipe Taper threads designed for a tighter seal. "T-NPTF" indicates National Pipe Taper Fuel threads for gas applications. "T-W" signifies Whitney threads, primarily used in fasteners. "T-Tr" is associated with tapered threads, typically in plumbing. "T-ACME" represents Acme threads for power transmission, and "T-STACME" indicates Stub Acme threads, which have a shorter profile for heavy loads.</p></p>
