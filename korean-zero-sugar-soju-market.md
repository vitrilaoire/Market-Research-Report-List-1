On the other hand, diluted soju combines distilled spirits with water and flavors, creating a lighter, sweeter profile at a lower alcohol content. Both types cater to health-conscious consumers looking for lower-calorie alcoholic options without sacrificing taste, driving their growing popularity in South Korea and beyond.</p></p>
