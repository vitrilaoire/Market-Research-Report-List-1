<p><p>The MBR membrane market consists of three primary types: tubular membranes, hollow fiber membranes, and flat membranes. Tubular membranes feature a cylindrical design that offers high flow rates and easy cleaning, making them suitable for large-scale applications. Hollow fiber membranes possess a high surface area in a compact form, ideal for wastewater treatment. Flat membranes, with their large, flat surface area, are effective in various filtration processes. Each type serves specific operational needs in water and wastewater treatment applications.</p></p>
