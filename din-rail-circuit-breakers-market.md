<p><p>The DIN Rail Circuit Breakers market comprises Thermal and Magnetic types. Thermal DIN Rail Circuit Breakers operate by detecting heat generated by excessive current, providing protection by tripping when current exceeds a preset limit. They are suitable for overcurrent protection in various applications. Magnetic DIN Rail Circuit Breakers, on the other hand, react quickly to short circuits using magnetic fields to trigger tripping instantly, ensuring rapid disconnection. Together, these breakers enhance safety and reliability in electrical distribution systems.</p></p>
