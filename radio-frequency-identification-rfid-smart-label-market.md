<p><strong>The Radio Frequency Identification (RFID) Smart Label Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ultra-High Frequency</li><li>High Frequency</li><li>Low Frequency</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The RFID smart label market is categorized into three frequency types: Ultra-High Frequency (UHF), High Frequency (HF), and Low Frequency (LF). UHF labels operate between 300 MHz and 3 GHz, facilitating long-range scanning and high data transfer rates, making them ideal for large inventory and supply chain applications. HF labels, operating at 13.56 MHz, are commonly used in access control and contactless payments due to their moderate read range. LF labels, usually around 125 kHz, are primarily utilized in animal tracking and asset management, offering strong penetration through materials but shorter read distances.</p></p>
