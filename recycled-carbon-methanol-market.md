<p><p>The Recycled Carbon Methanol market comprises two main types: Bio Methanol and E-Methanol. Bio Methanol is produced from biomass, utilizing organic materials like agricultural waste or wood, effectively converting carbon from these sources into sustainable methanol. In contrast, E-Methanol is generated using renewable electricity to convert carbon dioxide into methanol through processes like electrolysis. Both types contribute to reducing greenhouse gas emissions and promote a circular economy, supporting the transition towards cleaner energy solutions.</p></p>
