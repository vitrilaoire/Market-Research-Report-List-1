<p><ul><li>Conventional Telescopic Shock Absorbers</li><li>Strut Type Shock Absorbers</li><li>Spring Seat Shock Absorbers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive electric shock absorber market includes several types designed to enhance vehicle performance and comfort. Conventional telescopic shock absorbers use hydraulic fluid for damping, effectively managing bounce during motion. Strut type shock absorbers integrate the spring and damper into a single unit, providing structural support and improved handling. Spring seat shock absorbers feature a coil spring mounted on the damper, enhancing suspension characteristics. Each type plays a crucial role in optimizing ride quality, stability, and vehicle dynamics.</p></p>
