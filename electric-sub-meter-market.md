<p><p>The electric sub-meter market is divided into two main types: single electric sub-meters and three-phase electric sub-meters. Single electric sub-meters are designed for residential or small commercial use, measuring energy consumption for individual units or apartments, providing accurate billing and energy management. In contrast, three-phase electric sub-meters cater to industrial or larger commercial applications, monitoring power usage across multiple phases for efficient energy distribution, enhanced performance, and advanced monitoring capabilities, allowing for better management of high-demand electrical systems.</p></p>
