<p><p>The Haemophilus influenzae nucleic acid detection kit market primarily comprises two types: Dry PCR and Fluorescence PCR. Dry PCR utilizes temperature cycling to amplify specific DNA sequences without the need for a liquid medium, allowing for easier transportation and storage. In contrast, Fluorescence PCR employs fluorescent dyes to monitor the amplification process in real-time, providing rapid results and enhanced sensitivity. Both types are essential for accurately diagnosing infections caused by Haemophilus influenzae, aiding in timely treatment and outbreak management.</p></p>
