In contrast, Resin Pellet Infusers use specialized resins that selectively bind to specific substances in the bloodstream, allowing for targeted removal of harmful compounds. Both devices serve critical roles in therapeutic procedures, improving patient outcomes by efficiently purifying blood during various medical conditions.</p></p>
