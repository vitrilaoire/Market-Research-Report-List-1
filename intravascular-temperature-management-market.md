<p><strong>The Intravascular Temperature Management Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Warming</li><li>Cooling</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Intravascular Temperature Management (ITM) encompasses two primary market types: warming and cooling. The warming segment focuses on increasing patients' core temperatures, primarily used in hypothermia treatment or surgical settings to prevent heat loss. Conversely, the cooling market aims to lower core body temperature for therapeutic reasons, such as managing fever or preventing neurological damage after cardiac arrest. Both approaches are designed to optimize patient outcomes by managing body temperature effectively during medical interventions.</p></p>
