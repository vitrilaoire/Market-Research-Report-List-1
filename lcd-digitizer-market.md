<p><ul><li>Passive Digitizer</li><li>Active Digitizer</li><li>Hybrid Digitizer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The LCD digitizer market comprises three main types: passive, active, and hybrid digitizers. Passive digitizers rely on touch-sensitive technology that detects pressure, often used in resistive touch screens. Active digitizers utilize electromagnetic or capacitive technologies, providing improved precision and requiring a stylus, ideal for graphic design or detailed work. Hybrid digitizers combine features of both, offering versatility for various applications such as smartphones and tablets, catering to users who need both touch and precision input capabilities.</p></p>
