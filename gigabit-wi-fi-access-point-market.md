Wireless Access Points are network devices that enable wireless connectivity by connecting to wired networks, facilitating high-speed internet access for devices. Wireless LAN Controllers manage multiple access points, streamlining configuration, security policies, and traffic distribution, ensuring optimal network performance and reliability. Together, they enhance wireless coverage and improve user experience in various environments like businesses, schools, and public spaces.</p></p>
