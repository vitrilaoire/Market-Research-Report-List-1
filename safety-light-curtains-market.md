<p><p>Safety light curtains are crucial in safeguarding machinery operations and ensuring worker safety. The market is segmented into PNP output, NPN output, and OSE output types. PNP output devices provide a positive voltage signal when interrupted, suitable for applications needing compatible connections with PNP sensors. NPN output devices work with negative voltage signals, ideal for systems favoring this configuration. OSE output, or open safety output, allows enhanced flexibility in integration, enabling users to customize safety monitoring solutions per application requirements.</p></p>
