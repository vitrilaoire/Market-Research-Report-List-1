Monoglycerols and diacylglycerols are important as emulsifiers and energy sources. Fatty acids are fundamental for energy storage and metabolic functions. The "others" category includes less common lipids with specialized applications in pharmaceuticals, food, and cosmetics, reflecting a diverse market landscape.</p></p>
