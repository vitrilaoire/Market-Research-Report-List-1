<p><p>The optical glass filters market consists of three main types: UV filters, visible filters, and infrared filters. UV filters block ultraviolet light, protecting sensitive materials and enhancing color fidelity in photography. Visible filters manipulate light in the visible spectrum, altering color balance and contrast for optical applications. Infrared filters block or transmit specific infrared wavelengths, crucial for thermal imaging and night vision technologies. Together, these filters serve diverse industries, including photography, optical instruments, and telecommunications, enhancing performance and image quality.</p></p>
