<p><p>The cordless telephone market is primarily segmented into three types: Analog, DECT, and other digital technologies. Analog cordless phones transmit voice signals over radio frequencies, often with limited range and susceptibility to interference. DECT (Digital Enhanced Cordless Telecommunications) offers enhanced security, clarity, and range by using digital signals, making it the preferred choice for residential and business use. Other digital technologies include systems like Wi-Fi or Bluetooth cordless phones, which utilize internet connectivity or short-range wireless communication for enhanced features and versatility.</p></p>
