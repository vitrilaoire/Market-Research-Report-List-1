<p><ul><li>Measuring Current Transformer</li><li>Protective Current Transformer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dry current transformer market is divided into two primary types: measuring current transformers and protective current transformers. Measuring current transformers are designed to provide accurate current measurements for monitoring and control applications in electrical systems. In contrast, protective current transformers are employed to detect fault conditions and ensure the safety of electrical equipment by isolating faults. Both types play crucial roles in enhancing the reliability, efficiency, and safety of electrical networks, catering to various industry needs.</p></p>
