<p><p>Medication adherence packaging systems are designed to improve patient compliance in taking prescribed medications. Unit-dose packaging systems provide single doses of medication in individually sealed packages, enhancing accuracy and convenience for patients. This method is particularly beneficial for those requiring precise dosages. In contrast, multi-dose packaging systems group several doses together, often in blister packs, making it easier for patients to manage multiple medications over time. Both systems aim to reduce medication errors and enhance overall treatment outcomes.</p></p>
