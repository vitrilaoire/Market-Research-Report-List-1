<p><p>Plasma spray coatings are a thermal spray process used to apply protective or functional coatings to various surfaces. The process involves heating powdered or wire feedstock materials to a molten or semi-molten state using a plasma torch and then propelling them onto a substrate. This creates a durable, high-performance coating that enhances properties such as wear resistance, thermal insulation, corrosion protection, and electrical conductivity. Plasma spray coatings are widely used in industries like aerospace, automotive, energy, and healthcare.</p><p>The Plasma Spray Coatings Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 10.5% during the forecast period. This growth is driven by increasing demand for advanced materials in industries such as aerospace, where coatings improve component durability and performance. Additionally, the healthcare industry is adopting plasma spray coatings for medical implants, further boosting market expansion.</p><p>Emerging trends include the development of eco-friendly coatings and the use of advanced materials like ceramics and composites.
