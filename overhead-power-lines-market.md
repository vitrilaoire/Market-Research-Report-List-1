<p><strong>The Overhead Power Lines Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Low voltage (Below 1000 V)</li><li>Medium voltage (1 KV- 100 KV)</li><li>High voltage (Above 100 KV)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Overhead power lines are categorized into three market types based on voltage levels. Low voltage lines, operating below 1000 volts, primarily supply residential and commercial areas. Medium voltage lines, ranging from 1 kV to 100 kV, are used for distribution networks, connecting substations to consumers. High voltage lines, exceeding 100 kV, facilitate long-distance transmission of electricity between generation plants and substations, minimizing energy loss. Each type plays a crucial role in efficiently delivering electrical power across various applications.</p></p>
