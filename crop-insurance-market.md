<p><ul><li>Crop Yield Insurance</li><li>Crop Price Insurance</li><li>Crop Revenue Insurance</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Crop insurance is categorized into three main types: Crop Yield Insurance, which protects against losses due to reduced production caused by natural events; Crop Price Insurance, which safeguards farmers against price declines of their crops, ensuring a minimum income; and Crop Revenue Insurance, which combines both yield and price elements, offering protection against income loss due to either lower yields or falling market prices. Together, these insurance types provide comprehensive financial support to farmers facing agricultural risks.</p></p>
