<p><ul><li>Online Remote Sensing</li><li>Offline Sensing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Onboard Temperature Sensing Devices market comprises two main types: Online Remote Sensing and Offline Sensing. Online Remote Sensing involves continuous, real-time monitoring of temperature data, allowing for immediate analysis and action in response to temperature variations. This method is often utilized in applications like industrial processes and environmental monitoring. In contrast, Offline Sensing involves periodic data collection, where temperature readings are taken at specified intervals, suitable for applications requiring less frequent monitoring, such as routine inspections or post-event analyses.</p></p>
