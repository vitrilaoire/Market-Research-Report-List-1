<p><ul><li>Natural Cocoa Powder</li><li>Alkalized/Dutch-process Cocoa Powder</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cocoa powder market comprises two main types: natural cocoa powder and alkalized (Dutch-process) cocoa powder. Natural cocoa powder is derived from raw cocoa beans and retains a more intense chocolate flavor with a slightly acidic taste, making it ideal for recipes that require leavening agents. Alkalized cocoa powder undergoes a processing method that neutralizes its acidity, resulting in a milder flavor and darker color, often preferred in baking and chocolate products for a smoother taste and consistency.</p></p>
