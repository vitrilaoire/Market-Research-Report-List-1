<p><p>The autoimmunity reagents market comprises various diagnostic methods, including BLOT, ELISA, IFA, and CLIA. BLOT techniques, such as Western blotting, detect specific proteins through gel electrophoresis, while ELISA (Enzyme-Linked Immunosorbent Assay) quantifies antibodies or antigens in samples using enzyme-linked detection. IFA (Immunofluorescence Assay) employs fluorescently labeled antibodies for visualizing autoantigens in tissue sections. CLIA (Chemiluminescent Immunoassay) utilizes luminescent reactions to quantify biomolecules, offering high sensitivity and faster results, making it vital for diagnosing autoimmune diseases.</p></p>
